Ping Cao

Hi, I am a PhD candidate at Maastricht University, the Netherlands.

My research focuses on developing AI methods for assisted reproduction. With a background in Medicine and a self-taught specialization in Machine Learning, I work at the intersection of clinical insight and computational innovation. My interests include AI, LLM, embryo imaging, reproductive genetics, and PGT.
