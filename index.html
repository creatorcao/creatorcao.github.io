Dr. Ping Cao

Hi, I'm Dr. Ping Cao, a Postdoc Fellow (Kootstra Talent Fellowship) at Maastricht University, the Netherlands.

My research focuses on developing AI methods for assisted reproduction. With a background in Medicine and a self-taught specialization in Machine Learning, I work at the intersection of clinical insight and computational innovation. My interests include AI, LLMs, embryo imaging, reproductive genetics, and preimplantation genetic testing.
